# Core judge demo (about 3 minutes)

## 1. Employee discovery

One-click sign in as `employee@perkline.demo`.

On **Home**, point out:

- 18,500 ALL remaining employer allowance;
- Friday Drops and live Tirana activity;
- recommended multi-provider Routes;
- the internal Perkline Credit: allowance/vouchers only, never employee cash.

Open **Perkline Match** and use:

> I’m stressed and want something relaxing under 12,000 ALL this weekend.

Show the ranked catalog-only result, one-sentence “Why this fits,” policy state, provider breakdown
and 9,200 ALL total. Submit it.

## 2. Company approval

Sign out and one-click sign in as `admin@perkline.demo`.

Open **Requests**. Show:

- employee and Route detail;
- current policy checks;
- three provider splits;
- approve/reject reason;
- bulk triage for multiple requests.

Approve the request and narrate the routing animation:

`AlbaTech → Motive Studio + Nova Recovery + Green Plate Blloku`

Funds are simulated directly to providers; the employee never receives cash.

## 3. Settlement proof

Open **Settlements**:

- “How payments work” flow;
- canonical bank/PayPal/hosted-processor destination types;
- masked bank details and no raw card form;
- provider settlement ledger.

Open the public **Provider portal** to show the received split and voucher confirmation.

## 4. Employee payoff

Return as Employee and open **Vouchers**. Show QR/code, provider, expiry, redemption steps and support.

Close with:

> Perkline makes welfare feel like local discovery, while TeamSystem keeps budget, policy and provider
> settlement controlled. Built for Tirana today; market, currency and locale contracts are ready for
> the next country.

## Reliability fallback

- Perkline Match is deterministic by default and never requires internet.
- Every package is rebuilt from catalog IDs server-side.
- `/api/reset` restores the seeded demo.
- Rewards payouts are hidden from the default demo and cannot confuse the welfare story.
